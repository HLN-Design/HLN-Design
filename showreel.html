Working in broadcast news requires precision under pressure, strong editorial judgement, and an ability to translate complex information into visuals that are accessible to a broad audience. Over four years at Nine, I have delivered graphics for live and breaking news coverage across the network, consistently meeting immovable deadlines and supporting some of the most significant news events of the day.

I thrive in fast-paced editorial environments where clarity, accuracy, and collaboration are essential, and where design plays a critical role in helping audiences understand what matters.
