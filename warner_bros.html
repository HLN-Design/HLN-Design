Warner Brothers Acquisition.

This explainer visualised Netflix's proposed acquisition of Warner Bros, focusing on the scale and implications of media consolidation.

To make complex financial and corporate data more accessible, the design adopted a familiar user-interface visual language inspired by streaming platforms. Clear typographic systems and modular layouts were used to break down figures and relationships in a way that was intuitive for a general audience.

This graphic simplified a massive corporate merger, using familiar visual language to make the business implications immediately understood by a general audience.
